## Important Notes

### Coverage Territory
- Health Insurance Platinum (80MB) and Health Insurance Beyond Platinum (100MB) are sold through various channels including agency, bancassurance, Health Insurance Platinum Plus, Beyond Platinum (additional benefit rider), Health Insurance Classic, Beyond Platinum Classic (additional benefit rider)
- Coverage is provided for treatment at all hospitals determined by the company in Thailand
- Service providers guarantee benefits only during illness that occurs after 30 days from effective date, except for accidents
- Benefits are paid according to Health Insurance Platinum (80MB) or Health Insurance Beyond Platinum (100MB) plan, combined with the maximum annual benefit amount stated on the certificate
- For treatment abroad, coverage excludes Royal Angkor International Hospital and Royal Phnom Penh Hospital

### Policy Terms
- Rider policy for additional coverage can be cancelled or terminated after claim settlement according to policy terms, by notification to the insured as per specified conditions
- Agent/Broker rights expire according to regulations; any changes will not require prior notice
- Benefits help reduce risk and expenses; actual coverage depends on company consideration regarding treatment necessity and reasonableness according to medical standards
- Premium payment is for rider coverage only; the rider must be attached to a main life policy or term policy according to company regulations
- Coverage for physical therapy/rehabilitation, hearing aids, respirators for home use, oxygen equipment, patient transport vehicles, crutches, wheelchairs is limited to cases where it is necessary as standard medical treatment or when patient becomes permanently disabled
- Premium charged is for rider only; this is in addition to the main policy; any benefits stated are insurance benefits only; for coverage scope and other details, please consider carefully before purchasing

### Premium and Tax
- Information in this document is a summary only; please read the application form carefully for scope of coverage, conditions, and exclusions before purchasing insurance
