## Overview

**Plan Name:** My First Class @ BDMS
**Tagline:** First Class Experience with World Class Health Care Protection and Services

### Maximum Annual Benefit
- **Platinum Plan:** Up to **120 million Baht** per policy year
- **Platinum Plan (60MB):** Up to **60 million Baht** per policy year

### Key Highlights

- Comprehensive health coverage with world-class medical team and innovative treatments at international standard hospitals/clinics within the BDMS network in Thailand and Cambodia
- Covers all medical treatments including IPD, OPD, dental, maternity, eye care, surgery, lab, X-ray, dialysis, chemotherapy, targeted cancer therapy, emergency accidents within 24 hours, and psychiatric treatment

### Preventive Care Coverage
- **Vaccination:** Up to **80,000 Baht** per policy year
- **Annual Health Checkup:** Up to **30,000 Baht** (1 time per policy year)

*Note: Benefits can be used after the policy has been effective for more than 12 months*

### Care Services Throughout Illness
- Medical Evacuation Service
- Concierge Service
- Nursing Care Service (Home Recovery)

*Special Privilege: Care services for Allianz Ayudhya customers. Interested parties can scan QR code for details. Terms, conditions, and service providers may change or cancel services without prior notice.*

### BDMS Network Hospitals
- Bangkok Hospital

- Phyathai Hospital
- Paolo Hospital
