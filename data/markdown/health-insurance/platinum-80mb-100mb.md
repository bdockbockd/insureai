## No Claim Period (Claim Interval)

### Between Group 1 and/or Group 2 Claims
- **Waiting Period:** 1 year from the date of previous diagnosis
- The rider will not pay benefits if a Group 1 or Group 2 critical illness is diagnosed within 1 year of the previous Group 1 critical illness claim

### Re-diagnosed CI (Recurrence of Same Disease)

| Disease | Waiting Period for Re-diagnosis |
|---------|--------------------------------|
| 1.1 Invasive Cancer | 2 years from first diagnosis date |
| 1.2 Acute Myocardial Infarction | 1 year from first diagnosis date |
| 1.3 Stroke | 1 year from first diagnosis date |

**Note:** For Re-diagnosed CI claims, the No Claim Period does not apply between claims.

## Premium Waiver Benefit

Upon first diagnosis of a **Group 1 Critical Illness**, the insured receives:
- **Waiver of premium payments** for this Critical Illness Multi Care rider
- **Continued coverage** without payment obligation
- Premium waiver applies from the premium due date after diagnosis
- Premiums paid more than 1 year before written notification are not eligible for waiver

## Benefit Payment Rules

### Same Cause/Organ Rule
If the insured is eligible for benefits under multiple provisions (#1, #2, and/or #4) due to:
- Same cause
- Same organ
- Same side of paired organs
- Diagnosed at the same time
- Surgery at the same time
- Same accident

**The company will pay only the highest benefit amount.**

### Same Category Rule
The company will **not pay Group 2 benefits** if the Group 2 disease is in the same disease category as a Group 1 disease that has already been paid.

### Death Before Payment
If the insured is eligible for benefits but dies before payment, benefits will be paid to the beneficiary, minus any outstanding debts.

## Exclusions

### For Critical Illness Claims
The following are excluded from coverage:

1. **Pre-existing Conditions:**
   - Abnormalities or diseases that existed before the coverage effective date
   - Conditions existing before contract reinstatement
   - Conditions existing before sum assured increase approval
   - *Exception:* If disclosed to and accepted by the company without exclusion

2. **Self-Inflicted:**
   - Insured refuses medical treatment, recommendations, or advice from physician

3. **Substance Abuse:**
   - Inhaling, eating, drinking, injecting, or introducing toxic substances into the body, whether conscious or mentally impaired

### For Total Permanent Disability Claims
1. Suicide, attempted suicide, or self-harm
2. Injury while committing a serious crime, during arrest, or escaping arrest
3. While under the influence of drugs or narcotics to the point of losing control
4. Pre-existing physical defects, illness, or injury not disclosed at application
5. Disability from bodily harm during pregnancy

### For Death Claims
1. Suicide within 1 year from coverage effective date
2. Murder by beneficiary with intent
