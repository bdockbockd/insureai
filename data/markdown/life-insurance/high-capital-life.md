## Dividend Information

### Death Dividend (from Policy Year 6 onwards)

If the insured dies from policy year 6 onwards, the company may consider paying a death dividend to the beneficiary. The dividend depends on:
- Investment returns throughout the policy period
- Company's terms, conditions, and dividend calculation methods
- Economic conditions and circumstances at the time

### Maturity Dividend

The company may consider paying a maturity dividend to the insured if they survive until policy maturity. The dividend depends on:
- Investment returns throughout the policy period
- Company's terms, conditions, and dividend calculation methods
- Economic conditions and circumstances at the time

## Exclusions (Coverage Exceptions)

### 1. Misrepresentation or Non-Disclosure
If there is non-disclosure or misrepresentation, the company may void the policy within **2 years** from:
- The policy effective date, OR
- The date of coverage renewal, OR
- The date of age correction, OR
- The date the company approves an increase in sum assured

The increase applies only to the additional sum assured portion.

### 2. Suicide
If the insured commits suicide within **1 year** from:
- The policy effective date, OR
- The date coverage takes effect due to age renewal, OR
- The date coverage takes effect due to reinstatement to original status of the last policy, OR
- The date the company approves an increase in sum assured

The benefit is limited only to the increased sum assured portion, except in cases of:
- Death of the insured where the beneficiary intentionally caused the death
