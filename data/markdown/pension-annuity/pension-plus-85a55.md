## Plan Overview

This is a tax-deductible pension/annuity plan designed to provide retirement income security. The plan offers guaranteed pension payments from age 55 to age 85, with the opportunity for additional pension benefits based on accumulated special sum assured (if applicable).

### Key Highlights

- **Pension Payment Period:** Receive pension from age 55 to age 85
- **Guaranteed Annual Pension:** 10% of Sum Assured every year
- **Bonus Opportunity:** Additional 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any)
- **Tax Benefits:** Premiums are tax-deductible (subject to Revenue Department regulations)
- **Coverage Period:** Until age 85
- **Premium Payment Period:** Until age 55

## Pension Benefit Structure

### Annual Pension Payment Schedule

The pension benefits begin when the insured reaches age 55 and continue until age 85.

| Policy Year | Age | Guaranteed Pension | Bonus Pension Opportunity |
|-------------|-----|-------------------|---------------------------|
| 1 | 55 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 2 | 56 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 3 | 57 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 4 | 58 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 5 | 59 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 6 | 60 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| ... | ... | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |
| 30 | 84 | 10% of Sum Assured | 10% of Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any) |

**Total Pension Payment Years:** 31 years (from age 55 to age 85)

### Pension Calculation Formula

**Annual Pension = 10% x Sum Assured + 10% x Accumulated Special Sum Assured (if any)**

## Accumulated Special Sum Assured Explanation

### Definition
The **Accumulated Special Sum Assured** (จำนวนเงินเอาประกันภัยเพิ่มพิเศษสะสม) is a form of dividend converted into additional sum assured, accumulated from the first year the company considers adding special sum assured to the policyholder.

### Special Sum Assured Determination
- The company may consider providing dividends in the form of Special Sum Assured
- If considered, it will begin from the **start of Policy Year 3** onwards
- The dividend converted to Special Sum Assured depends on investment returns allocated by the company to the policyholder
- Returns may vary according to the insurance plan type
- Subject to conditions, terms, and calculation methods determined by the company each year

## Life Coverage Benefits

### Pre-Pension Period (Before Age 55)

#### Policy Year 1-2
Upon death, the beneficiary receives the **higher** of:
- 100% of total main premiums paid*, OR
- Cash surrender value

#### Policy Year 3 to Before Age 55
Upon death, the beneficiary receives the **higher** of:
- 110% of total main premiums paid*, OR
- Cash surrender value

### During Pension Period (Age 55-85)

Upon death, the beneficiary receives the **higher** of:
- Total main premiums paid* minus all pension payments (guaranteed portion) already paid to the insured, OR
- Present value according to the Present Value Table of Pension (in case of death before receiving pension for 15 years)

**Note:** *Does not include premiums for additional premium loading, endorsements, and/or other riders (if any)
