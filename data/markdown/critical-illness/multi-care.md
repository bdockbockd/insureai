## Key Features

### Coverage Highlights

- **Total Diseases/Conditions Covered**: Up to 81 diseases/conditions
- **Maximum Total Payout**: 840% of sum assured
- **Maximum Claims**: Up to 13 times throughout the contract
- **Re-claim Feature**: Available for 3 top critical illnesses (Re-diagnosed CI)
- **Coverage Age**: From childhood to elderly
- **Renewal**: Can renew coverage until age 99

### Premium Waiver Benefit

If diagnosed with a Group 1 Critical Illness for the first time, the policyholder receives **premium waiver** for this rider while continuing to receive coverage.

### Death Benefit

- **Payout**: 100% of sum assured
- **Deduction**: Minus all benefits already paid under Items 1 and 2 (if any)

## Waiting Period

### Standard Waiting Period: 60 Days

The rider does not cover critical illness or abnormalities that doctors confirm and have clear evidence relating to covered critical illnesses for:

- **Group 1 Diseases**: 1.1 - 1.6
- **Group 2 Diseases**: 2.1 - 2.20
- **Benefits**: Items 3, 4, and 5

If the condition occurs within **60 days** from the effective date of coverage.

---

## No Claim Period

### Between Claims: 1 Year

The rider will not provide coverage if the diagnosis date for Group 1 or Group 2 Critical Illness occurs within **1 year** from the date of the last Group 1 Critical Illness diagnosis for which benefits were paid.

### Claim Limits

- **Group 1 Claims**: Maximum 1 time per disease (except Re-diagnosed CI)
- **Total Group 1 Claims**: Maximum 6 times throughout the contract (including renewals)

---

## Re-diagnosed CI (Recurrence Claims)

For the following 3 diseases, policyholders can claim again if the condition recurs:

### 1.1 Invasive Cancer - Re-claim Conditions

- **Waiting Period**: 2 years from the first claim diagnosis date
- **Required Evidence**:
  - Pathology report confirming recurrence
  - Radiology and/or laboratory results
  - Treatment with radiation, chemotherapy, or surgery to support recurrence diagnosis
- **Exclusion**: Preventive treatments including Tamoxifen or Raloxifene are NOT covered

### 1.2 Acute Myocardial Infarction - Re-claim Conditions

- **Waiting Period**: 1 year from the first claim diagnosis date

### 1.3 Stroke - Re-claim Conditions

- **Waiting Period**: 1 year from the first claim diagnosis date

**Note**: The No Claim Period does not apply to Re-diagnosed CI claims.

## Policy Termination Conditions

The rider terminates when:

1. Benefits under Item 1 (Group 1 Critical Illness) have been paid 6 times in total (including Re-diagnosed CI claims)
2. The policyholder dies
3. The main policy terminates

---

## Special Payment Rules

### Same Cause/Organ Rule

If the policyholder is entitled to receive benefits under Items 1, 2, and/or 4 from:
- The same cause
- The same organ
- The same side of an organ
- Diagnosis at the same time
- Surgery at the same time
- Same accident

The company will pay **only the highest benefit** amount (one item only).

### Death Before Payment

If the policyholder is entitled to benefits under this rider but dies before payment, the company will pay the benefits to the beneficiary as stated in the policy, minus any outstanding debts (if any).

### Group 2 Exclusion Rule

The company will NOT pay Group 2 benefits if the Group 2 Critical Illness is in the same disease category as a Group 1 Critical Illness for which benefits have already been paid (as per the specified list).

## Exclusions

### For Critical Illness Claims

The following are NOT covered:

1. **Pre-existing Conditions**: Any abnormality that doctors confirm and have clear evidence relating to critical illness or that occurred before:
   - The effective date of coverage
   - The reinstatement date (if the rider lapsed)
   - The date the company approved an increase in sum assured
   - **Exception**: Unless the policyholder disclosed the condition and the company accepted the risk without exclusion

2. **Refusal of Treatment**: If the policyholder refuses to accept treatment, recommendations, or follow doctor's advice

3. **Self-Poisoning**: Inhaling, eating, drinking, injecting, or introducing toxic substances into the body by any means, whether mentally competent or not

### For Total Permanent Disability Claims

1. **Self-Harm**: Suicide, attempted suicide, or self-inflicted bodily harm

2. **Criminal Activity**: Injury while committing a serious crime, during arrest, or fleeing arrest

3. **Substance Abuse**: While under the influence of narcotics or addictive substances to the point of losing control

4. **Undisclosed Pre-existing Conditions**: Physical defects, illness, or injury that the policyholder knew about at the time of application but did not disclose

### For Death Benefit Claims

1. **Suicide**: If the policyholder commits suicide voluntarily within 1 year from the effective date of the rider

2. **Murder by Beneficiary**: If the policyholder is intentionally killed by the beneficiary
