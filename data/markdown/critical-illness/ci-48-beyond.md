| Benefit Received | Contract Status |

## Important Notes and Conditions

### Benefit Payment Rules

1. **Percentage (%)** refers to percentage of Sum Assured

2. **60-Day Waiting Period**: The company will pay benefits for illnesses occurring after 60 days from the effective date of the rider for certain critical illnesses specified in the policy

3. **Single Claim for Groups 1 & 2**: If the insured is diagnosed with more than one critical illness from Group 1 or Group 2, the company will pay benefits for only one disease

4. **One-Time Payment for Item 2**: Benefits under item 2 (Groups 3, 4, 5) will be paid only once throughout the policy term, even if the contract is renewed

5. **Same Organ Rule**: If the insured is entitled to benefits from Group 1 and/or Group 2 and/or Group 3 and/or Group 5 for the same organ, same side of organ, diagnosed at the same time, operated at the same time, or resulting from the same accident, the company will pay only the highest benefit amount

6. **Death Before Payment**: If the insured is entitled to benefits under this rider and dies before payment, the company will pay the benefits to the beneficiary as specified in the policy

## Exclusions

### For Critical Illness Claims

The following causes are excluded from coverage:

1. **Poisoning**: Inhaling, eating, drinking, injecting, or introducing toxic substances into the body by any method, whether conscious or not, sane or insane

2. **Refusal of Treatment**: The insured refuses to accept treatment, recommendations, or follow medical advice

### For Total Permanent Disability Claims

The following causes are excluded from coverage:

1. **Criminal Activity**: Injury while the insured is committing a serious crime, or while being arrested, or fleeing from arrest

2. **Under Influence**: While under the influence of narcotics or illegal drugs to the extent of being unable to maintain consciousness
