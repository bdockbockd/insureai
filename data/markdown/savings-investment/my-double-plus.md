## Key Highlights

### Double Coverage Benefit
- **Standard Annual Benefit**: Up to **30 million Baht** per policy year (Plan 3)
- **Double Benefit Trigger**: Upon first diagnosis of 1 of 10 critical illnesses
- **Enhanced Annual Benefit**: Up to **60 million Baht** per policy year (doubled)
- **Double Benefit Duration**: 4 consecutive policy years following diagnosis

### Comprehensive Coverage
- **Pay-as-actual** medical expenses including:
  - X-ray, Laboratory tests
  - Surgery, Dialysis
  - Chemotherapy
  - Targeted Therapy for cancer treatment
  - Emergency accident treatment within 24 hours
  - ICU treatment

### Post-Hospitalization Care
- Rehabilitation medicine (เวชศาสตร์ฟื้นฟู)
- Follow-up appointments (wound care, stitch removal, etc.)

### Preventive Care
- Vaccination and/or Annual health check-up benefit
- Up to **5,500 Baht** per policy year (Plan 3)
- Requires policy to be in force for more than 12 months

## 10 Critical Illnesses Covered (for Double Benefit)

The double benefit is triggered upon first diagnosis of any of these 10 critical illnesses:

| # | Critical Illness (English) | Critical Illness (Thai) |
|---|---------------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Acute Myocardial Infarction due to Ischemia | กล้ามเนื้อหัวใจตายเฉียบพลันจากการขาดเลือด |
| 2 | Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery | การผ่าตัดเส้นเลือดเลี้ยงกล้ามเนื้อหัวใจ |
| 3 | Open Heart Valve Surgery | การผ่าตัดลิ้นหัวใจโดยวิธีการเปิดหัวใจ |
| 4 | Cerebral Aneurysm Requiring Surgery | โรคหลอดเลือดสมองโป่งพองที่ต้องรักษาโดยการผ่าตัด |
| 5 | Stroke (Hemorrhagic or Ischemic) | โรคหลอดเลือดสมองแตกหรืออุดตัน |
| 6 | Invasive Cancer | โรคมะเร็งระยะลุกลาม |
| 7 | Organ Transplant or Bone Marrow Transplant | การผ่าตัดเปลี่ยนอวัยวะหรือปลูกถ่ายไขกระดูก |
| 8 | Aortic Surgery | การผ่าตัดเส้นเลือดแดงใหญ่เอออร์ต้า |
| 9 | Idiopathic Scoliosis Surgery | การผ่าตัดกระดูกสันหลังคดที่ไม่ทราบสาเหตุ |
| 10 | Major Burns | แผลไหม้ฉกรรจ์ |

> *Definitions of covered critical illnesses are as specified in the contract definitions section

### Double Benefit Example

**Scenario**: Plan 3 selected, policy effective from January 1, 2066 (BE 2566/2023 CE)
**Event**: Diagnosed with Stroke on June 1, 2066

| Policy Year | Period | Annual Benefit Limit |
|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| Year 1 | Jan 1, 2066 - Dec 31, 2066 | 60 million Baht (doubled from June 1) |
| Year 2 | Jan 1, 2067 - Dec 31, 2067 | 60 million Baht |
| Year 3 | Jan 1, 2068 - Dec 31, 2068 | 60 million Baht |
| Year 4 | Jan 1, 2069 - Dec 31, 2069 | 60 million Baht |
| Year 5 | Jan 1, 2070 - Dec 31, 2070 | 60 million Baht |
| Year 6 | Jan 1, 2071 onwards | 30 million Baht (returns to standard) |

**Key Points:**
- Double benefit starts from the date of first critical illness diagnosis
- Continues for 4 additional policy years
- Can only be triggered once per critical illness
- Daily and per-visit limits remain unchanged as per benefits table

## Exclusions (Sample)

The following are examples of exclusions from coverage:

### 1. Congenital and Genetic Conditions
- Conditions resulting from congenital abnormalities
- Incomplete organ development from birth
- Genetic diseases
- Abnormalities in body development
- **Exception**: Coverage available if policy has been in force for at least 1 year AND symptoms appear after insured reaches age 16

### 2. Experimental and Sleep-Related Treatments
- Treatments still under experimental/trial phase
- Examination or treatment for:
  - Sleep apnea
  - Sleep disorders
  - Snoring

### 3. Pregnancy-Related
- Pregnancy
- Miscarriage
- Abortion
- Childbirth
- Pregnancy complications
- Infertility treatment (including investigation and treatment)
- Sterilization or contraception
- **Exception**: Choriocarcinoma (molar pregnancy cancer) is covered

## Important Notes

1. **Marketing vs Official Name**: "ประกันสุขภาพ ปลดล็อค ดับเบิล แคร์" is the marketing name; the official contract name is "สัญญาเพิ่มเติมคุ้มครองสุขภาพ ปลดล็อค ดับเบิล แคร์" with additional endorsement for other benefits.

2. **Benefit Payment**: Total benefits after deductible (if any) cannot exceed the maximum annual benefit as specified in the benefits table.

3. **Premium Payment**: Premium payment is the insured's responsibility. Agent/broker collection is a service only.

4. **Premium Variation**: Premiums vary by:
   - Gender
   - Age bracket
   - Occupation
   - Plan selected
   - Renewal premiums change based on age and/or occupation
   - Subject to company's rules and guidelines

5. **Rider Premium Only**: Premiums shown in this document are for the Double Care rider only, excluding main policy premium.

6. **Supplementary Contract**: This product must be attached to a main policy as specified by the company.
