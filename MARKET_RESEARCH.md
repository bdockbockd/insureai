InsureAI is positioned to capture the growing digital insurance market in Thailand and Southeast Asia. With Allianz as the backing partner and AI-powered personalization, we aim to acquire 50,000+ users in Year 1.

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "Rising Professional"
- **Age**: 28-45
- **Income**: 40,000-100,000 THB/month
- **Location**: Bangkok, Chiang Mai, major cities
- **Behavior**: Mobile-first, uses LINE daily, researches before buying
- **Pain Points**: Confused by options, time-poor, wants quick decisions

### Secondary Persona: "Family Protector"
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Status**: Married with children
- **Income**: 80,000+ THB/month
- **Behavior**: Concerned about family security, compares options
- **Pain Points**: Wants comprehensive coverage, doesn't understand policies

### Tertiary Persona: "Digital Expat"
- **Age**: 30-60
- **Origin**: US, UK, EU, Japan, Korea
- **Behavior**: International coverage needs, premium-focused
- **Pain Points**: Language barrier, unfamiliar with local options
